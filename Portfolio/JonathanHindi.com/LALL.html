LA Law Library
Graphic Designer

The second largest legal library in the United States. I was brought on board to help redefine the library from being a warehouse of book to a leader in research technology.

Creating a new face for the library and implementing it throughout all mediums of communication was my task. Mediums such as print, including flyers, brochures, library cards, business cards, posters and banners. Digital media such as microsites, emails, patron kiosk and PowerPoint presentations.


Updated the website with news articles, classes, events and regular maintenance.


Email campaigns and alerts created to increase awareness and class registrations.
