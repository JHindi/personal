Cabulous
Project Manager

Cabulous is a startup company which provides an automated system that mediates between cab drivers/fleets and their passengers. It also provided a dispatch system and tracker for dispatchers and fleet owners. In this unique dynamics of integration, I was able to bring forth a dispatch system that was easier, faster and more comprehensive than what the cab industry currently used. I was also able to bring forth a user friendly app that allowed users to select rides directly and easily without needing to do anything more than to tap on a cab on their map. My responsibilities included:

  • Product development
  • Designing logic workflows
  • Designing native UX wireframes
  • Designing responsive platforms that embraced native tools
  • Front end development
  • QA and Support
