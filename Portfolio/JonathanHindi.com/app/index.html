    Interpreting the Enunciator Panel

    The OSPP is both a descriptive and prescriptive analysis tool. Managers can now assess which area(s) of the firm to concentrate it resources to improve its performance position. The enunciator panel indicates a green or red signal. Green indicates that the gap between the ETL and the assessed capability is within tolerance. Red indicates the gap between the ETL and the assessed capability is excessive, contributing to poor performance. Management now has the option to increase, modify, and replace the low performing descriptor factors to increase the score. As management moves through the various assessment screens making modifications, the OSPP matrix with reflect those management choices, thus adding to management's understanding of the relationship of the variables and descriptors affecting them.
